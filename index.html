The story of Unus Annus

Once uopn a time there was a youtube channel

This particular youtube channel was made by two goofy guys who decided to create this channel together, their goal was to create entertaining videos everyday for a year and then they would delete the channel and all it's content. Hence the name "Unus Annus" meaning "One year" in latin. Aside from the videos being minimum 10 minutes long, they also wanted to broaden their horizon of experiences and push their limits or "go even further beyond" as they said many times during different episodes.

Memento Mori

As the channel came to life it quickly became clear that they often brought up the subject of life and it's enevitable end; death. Reminding the audience that the clock was ticking ever onwards and time waits for no one. This reminder was also placed in the intro and outro of each episode, accompanied by the ticking sound of the clock and the numbers counting down the end of the channel itself. They added a lot of humor and made a comic bit about the channels death in the end of many of their episodes, after all they wanted to make people laugh, but also remind them to do what they wanted to do, as they wanted their viewers to keep in mind that life is too short to wait for the "proper time". And so many of their viewers took to their philosophy and saw many of their quotes, such as "memento mori", "time always seems to move faster when it's running out" and so on, as personal reminders to seize the day and live life to the fullest.

Theme and theories

This journey had a main theme of black and white, with many theories as to why, some claiming it meant white was life and black was death, others claiming it meant white was positive and black was negative, alongside many other theories. The two guys responsible for the channel wore suits, one black, the other white, as a way to step into character where they would refer to themselves as "Unus" and "Annus". Once their characters where established, roughly a month or two into the year, they started pinpointing the jokey bit about death to their characters dying.

Lore

While the episodes where very different in what content they contained, the theme of life, death and the never ending ticking of the clock was kept in all the episodes. Some episodes would be simple challenges, such as drawing old memes from memory, others would be trying new things, possibly things that are well known, but that the goofy guys themselves had never tried. In one episode they would see how many hot-dogs they could eat within a set time limit, in another they tried to create their own sensory deprivation chamber in a bathtub, followed by them going to an actual sensory deprivation chamber to compare their own attempt to the real thing. They also went casket shopping in an actual casket and coffin shop, where they designed the somewhat famous "Unus Annus coffin".
